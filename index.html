FAQs

Creating a profile is easy. After downloading the app, sign up and start by entering basic information about your cat, such as name, age, breed, and gender. You can then upload photos, videos, and write a bio describing your cat’s personality and interests. The more detailed and engaging your profile, the better the chances of finding a good match.

Our matching algorithm analyzes various factors such as your cat’s personality, energy levels, and preferences. It uses this data to suggest potential matches that are likely to be compatible. You can enhance the matching accuracy by providing detailed information in your cat’s profile and participating in the Purrsonality Quiz, which further refines the matchmaking process.

The Purrsonality Quiz is a fun and interactive quiz that helps identify your cat’s personality traits, such as whether they are more playful, relaxed, shy, or adventurous. The results of this quiz are factored into the matching algorithm to ensure that the suggested matches align well with your cat’s temperament and behavior.

Yes, TinCat allows you to manage profiles for multiple cats under one account. You can easily switch between profiles to view matches, message other cat owners, and arrange playdates for each of your cats individually.

When arranging an in-person playdate, TinCat provides a Playdate Planner tool that helps you schedule and organize the meetup. We also offer safety tips, such as choosing a neutral location, bringing familiar toys or treats, and monitoring the interaction closely. Always ensure that both cats are comfortable and safe throughout the playdate.
